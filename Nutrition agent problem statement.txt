Many people struggle to maintain a healthy diet because they lack accurate knowledge about the nutritional value of the foods they eat. Reading nutrition labels, estimating portion sizes, tracking calorie intake, and identifying nutrient deficiencies can be time-consuming and confusing. Existing solutions often require manual data entry, making them inconvenient for everyday use.

The challenge is to develop an AI-powered Nutrition Analyzer that can automatically identify food items from images or user input, estimate their nutritional content, calculate calories and macronutrients, and provide personalized dietary recommendations based on the user's health goals, age, weight, activity level, and medical conditions. The system should offer real-time insights, encourage healthier eating habits, and help users make informed dietary decisions with minimal effort.
